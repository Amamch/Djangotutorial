ABOUT US


Flickers-tech is a website specifically for news, updates, and articles around Programming, Hacking, Cybersecurity. It was founded on 5/01/2022 by Uzoigwe Amamchukwu(REMS) in his dorm @the University of Enugu Campus. Our Goal is to let users have not just the best of ecstatic experience, but also to be informed on things happening in the world of Programming, Hacking and Cybersecurity.
